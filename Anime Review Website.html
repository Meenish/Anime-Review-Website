One Piece is an epic adventure anime that follows Monkey D. Luffy, an enthusiastic young man whose body gained the properties of rubber after accidentally eating a mystical Devil Fruit. With his diverse crew of pirates, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King.
